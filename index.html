Our Story

Our love story began five years ago at a small coffee shop downtown. What started as a chance encounter over spilled coffee has blossomed into the greatest adventure of our lives.

From our first date at the local farmers market to traveling the world together, every moment has been filled with laughter, growth, and endless love. We've supported each other through challenges and celebrated countless joys.

Now, we're ready to take the next step in our journey together. We can't wait to celebrate with all of you - the friends and family who have made our story even more beautiful.
